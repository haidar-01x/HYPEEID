Our Goals

We are committed to building a thriving community through these core objectives

Promote Positive Engagement

Create an atmosphere where children and adults can actively participate in Eid festivities, fostering a sense of excitement and pride in their faith.

Educate and Inspire

Provide educational resources, workshops, and talks that deepen the understanding of Islamic teachings and values, motivating individuals to embrace their religion.

Build Strong Community Bonds

Foster a sense of unity and camaraderie among community members by organising communal events and facilitating opportunities for community service.

Empower the Youth

Engage and empower young individuals through age-appropriate activities, competitions, and mentorship programs that encourage their active involvement.

Amplify Cultural Heritage

Celebrate the rich cultural diversity within the Muslim community by highlighting traditional arts, crafts, and cuisines associated with Eid.

Collaborate and Network

Forge partnerships and collaborations with local mosques, community organisations, and individuals to create a collective and impactful effort.

The Hyped Eid Community aims to create a vibrant, engaging, and inclusive Eid experience that instils a deep love and appreciation for Islam, fosters a strong sense of community, and empowers individuals to embrace their faith and cultural heritage.
